# Limitations

- The score is a transparent rules-based preparation aid, not a predictor of financing outcomes.
- Demo comparison scores use documented calibration snapshots; user-company component and total scores are deterministic.
- Keyword classification cannot deeply interpret arbitrary contracts, legal documents, or unusual spreadsheets.
- Scanned PDFs require OCR, which is not included.
- Pipeline probabilities and concentration values are founder- or operator-provided.
- Confidence labels are heuristic coverage states, not probabilistic certainty.
- A text note about a SAFE, IP assignment, or filing does not automatically replace structured evidence.
- Data-room expectations are prototype defaults and are not a legal checklist.
- Generated Q&A and risk notes remain drafts until reviewed.
- There is no authentication, authorization, encryption, audit log, document versioning, or organization tenancy.
- There is no browser-test suite or ESLint configuration yet.

The application does not provide legal, tax, accounting, investment, fundraising, privacy, security, or compliance advice.
